Heaven On Earth Hotel Branding

Opened in December 2021 and nestled in the heart of Quy Nhon City, Heaven On Earth is a premium hotel brand, focuses on young people aged between 18 and 35 years old. Because they are traveling further and wider more than any other generation did before, with a focus on new experiences and authenticity.

Brainstorm

"The name Heaven On Earth is contemporary and brings to mind mood and tone, which define our brand. If you are looking for a hotel that is dreamy, vivid and contemporary, you have come to the right place. Conveniently located and equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, our services aim to Happy, Opulent and Entertain."

Define

The first “Z-Gen” lifestyle hotel in the city, Heaven On Earth aims to redefine the conventional hospitality experience with our contemporary, yet opulent services that offer our guests something truly unique. Designed to be ultra-modern with an infusion of indigenous design elements, Heaven On Earth aims to offer an unforgettable experience to all that stay with us.

Let's sketch

starting from scratch, I worked on naming, brand identity, key visual development, brand application, and managed the design process. My objective was to create a cohesive design concept that sets Heaven On Earth apart from every other hotel in Quy Nhon city.

now that we've cracked down the basics,
here's the good stuff that I made.
heaven on earth hotel branding

In the beginning, I wanted to create a hotel brand that is "unique", "dreamy", "unreal" and "aesthetic". But I also tended to love flowers and I thought a hotel with rose blooming would be stunning. It is like paradise, cloud... hmmm, salmon color cloud would be perfect, so sweet and dreamy.

I started researching about roses. I found heaven on earth rose, a salmon color flower from Germany, which perfectly matches my vision.

That's why I chose the name "Heaven on Earth" for my hotel brand.
