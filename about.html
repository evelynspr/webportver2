Biography

Tuyet Nhi (Evelyn) is a junior graphic designer and visual designer, based in Ho Chi Minh City.
Graduated Student at University of Finance and Marketing (Major: Marketing Communication) and Arena Multimedia (Major: Multimedia Designer)
Since 2021, she has been working as a Graphic Designer at ADAM Muzic Academy. Her personal projects were featured in Arena Multimedia design publications and their social media.
Her goal is to grow as a designer, continue to learn new skills to become an Art Director in the future.
