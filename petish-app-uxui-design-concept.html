Petish App Design Concept

Petish is an e-commerce application that will help users find and purchase the best products for their "pet friends". Besides, Petish is also a pet-lover community, sharing pet-caring knowledge and experiences.

Discover

The e-commerce market is growing rapidly day by day. In Vietnam, however, there is not a single platform for pets currently. Pet lovers are facing a lot of problems because there are too many e-commerce marketplaces which are too messy. In particular, they also need a trusted platform to share and learn new essential skills and knowledge.

Define

By creating a platform where users from 18-45 years old can build their own online shop, sell pet products, connect, buy, and share everything about pets, Petish assists users to purchase genuine products from the trusted shops. Moreover, users can learn and exchange knowledge to improve knowledge themselves remotely.

Taking into account the short scope of this project, user personas were created to quickly represent the users, their context's, needs, pain points, and motivations, as to inform the solution in the develop phase.

User personas

Let's develop

My mission in this project is to create a design concept and demonstrate some main functional screens.
Having used user personas to define the target users, the develop phase began. This aimed at creating initial solution ideas via sketching, outlining the solution flow through user flows and low fidelity prototyping to assess the proposed solution.

Simple and easy to use

I removed unnecessary details and functions and focus on the main function - shopping. The UI layout was designed in order to keep the focus on the products and their information. I also used an AI bot (the cat tail) throughout the app to create a stronger connection with users. They can turn on or off the AI bot in "Setting".

With the aesthetic/cute feeling being one of the highlights of the app, I also used the brand's colors to ensure Petish’s visual tone.
EVERYONE IS WELCOME TO JOIN us
a friendly and welcoming tone throughout the app

To create a delightful and reliable environment and increase user engagement, I created the Petish admins with different characteristics. They will represent the brand's tone of voice. Besides that, I used pastel hashtags and some badges to make users feel trusthworthy and happy.

In addition, by placing the post button ("+" button) at the right bottom of the screen, it is an easily actionable and comfortable position for the human thumb to interact with.

take away


In summary, under the dedicated and thorough guidance of my mentor Ta Thanh Khue (again), the Petish project allowed me to take a departure from creatively liberal design in order to place a greater emphasis on functionality.

The process of designing concept served to bring the solution to life and brought me closer to the real world problems that it set out to solve.

Throughout the process I was able to focus on designing for a wide variety of users (18-45y/o) and this was challenged to think about universal design principles to ensure clarity and ease of navigation so that the perceived complexity is minimal and users can focus on finding the best products and community for them.

Finally as a result of the project scope I feel that certain aspects were not fully explored, namely the Petish admin system and how they can monitor, manage high-quality posts in community.
