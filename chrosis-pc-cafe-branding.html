Chrosis PC Cafe Branding

Chrosis PC Cafe is an integrated brand of cyber gaming and cafeteria with Cyberpunk - Futuristic style. The brand name is shortened from "Chronostasis" with the meaning of stopping time at a moment. The logo name highlights our desire which brings a special and unique experience to the gaming community, as well as Cyberpunk lovers through a visit and using the services of Chrosis PC Cafe.

Brainstorm

Through out many ideas, we decided to incorporate something that is not in the traditional form. Chrosis's target customer is young people, so using light and dark colors is not only suitable for our brand ideas but also suitable for Cyber Cafe space. In this project, the cyberpunk aesthetic is determined by three markers: Shape – geometrical in particular. Cyberpunk colors – a typical cyberpunk color palette includes vibrant neon lights: cyberpunk pink, and blue. The use of neon color will create accents in Cyberpunk style in general as well as Chrosis PC Cafe in particular. Space – dark, underground, but real-world environments.

Define

As the slogan has been chosen, when coming to Chrosis PC Cafe, customers are frozen in reality to enter another world, a world of High Tech, Low Life" covered with neon colors. In Chrosis PC Cafe, customers can choose to become professional players with the most advanced equipment, or simply enjoy other services. Everything is wrapped up in the world of Chrosis.
