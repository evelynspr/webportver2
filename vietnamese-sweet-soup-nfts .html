Vietnamese Sweet Soup NFTs

Vietnamese Sweet soup Nfts is a project I had an opportunity to join while I was a intern designer at dinosaur vietnam.

Brainstorm

There is a wide variety of Ch√® in Vietnam. It is a decent dessert that brings happiness to all Vietnamese people at all ages. The dish may look simple but it has a surprising number of ingredients and topings, due to the variety in tastes and preferences. Therefore, inside each kind of sweet soup is a wonderful Vietnamese traditional story.

Define

The target audiences of Vietnamese Sweet Soup NFTs are tourists or fans of Vietnamese food who want to keep a memory of the food they have eaten. Through out many ideas, we decided to choose pixel art style (old but gold) and 2-shades art style (simple and mordern).
